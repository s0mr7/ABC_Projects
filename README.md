# ABC_Projects
Overview and Objective : 
This dashboard was developed to enable ABC Bookstore management to monitor operational and financial performance instantly. The primary goal is to extract quick, data-driven insights to inform decisions related to inventory management, pricing strategy, and category selection.

Tools Utilized: 
Data Cleaning: Power Query
Data Visualization: Tableau

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs): 
The dashboard provides a quick performance snapshot through the following core metrics:
Total Titles: 999
Total Quantity in Stock: 8,585
Average Rating (Avg. Rating) for all books: 3.00
Average Price (Avg. Price) for all books: $35
Quantity Per Book: 8.59

Key Sections and Insights
The dashboard is divided into key analytical sections designed to provide insights into:
Category Rating and Inventory:
The Top 8 Book Categories by Rating are identified to show which genres achieve the highest customer satisfaction (e.g., Adult Fiction and Erotica).
The Top 5 Book Categories by Quantity in Stock are displayed to pinpoint the genres occupying the largest volume of current inventory (e.g., Sequential Art and Fiction), which aids in warehouse management.
Pricing and Sales:
The dashboard highlights the Top 5 Most Expensive Categories and Titles (e.g., the Suspense category), assisting in reviewing pricing strategies and identifying high-value products.
The Top 10 Book Titles by Quantity in Stock are identified, illuminating books that may require promotional campaigns or are consistently high-volume items.
